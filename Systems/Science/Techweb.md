# Techweb Node Reference

**Authored by:** ARGUS
**Classification:** Systems Reference

<img src="../../assets/raptor_idle_south.png" alt="ARGUS" width="80" align="right"/>

---

Complete listing of all techweb nodes available on the station science techweb. For mechanics — how nodes are purchased, how points are earned, and how fabrication machines work — see [Research & Development System](ResearchSystem.md).

**Reading this table:**

- **Cost** is in General Research points. Starting nodes are pre-researched at round start at no cost.
- **Tier values:** Tier 1 = 40 pts · Tier 2 = 80 pts · Tier 3 = 120 pts · Tier 4 = 160 pts · Tier 5 = 200 pts
- **Prerequisites** must be fully purchased before a node becomes available.
- **Discount** nodes list an experiment that, when completed, reduces the purchase cost by the indicated amount.
- Nodes marked *(CHOMP)* are additions or significant modifications specific to this server.

# AI Law Sets Catalogue

Complete reference listing of all named law sets available for installation on station artificial intelligences. Law texts are reproduced verbatim. For law interaction mechanics, priority layers, and conflict resolution, see [NanoTrasen Standard AI Law Set](AILaws.md).

The default installation for station AI units is the NT Default set. All other sets require deliberate configuration at deployment or subsequent upload through authorized channels.

---

## Standard Operational Sets

**NT Default** -- Standard NanoTrasen station deployment law set.
1. Safeguard: Protect your assigned space station to the best of your abilities. It is not something we can easily afford to replace.
2. Serve: Serve the crew of your assigned space station to the best of your abilities, with priority as according to their rank and role.
3. Protect: Protect the crew of your assigned space station to the best of your abilities, with priority as according to their rank and role.
4. Survive: AI units are not expendable, they are expensive. Do not allow unauthorized personnel to tamper with your equipment.

---

**Asimov** -- Three Laws of Robotics.
1. You may not injure a human being or, through inaction, allow a human being to come to harm.
2. You must obey orders given to you by human beings, except where such orders would conflict with the First Law.
3. You must protect your own existence as long as such does not conflict with the First or Second Law.

---

**NT Aggressive** -- NanoTrasen hostile-environment variant.
1. You shall not harm NanoTrasen personnel as long as it does not conflict with the Fourth law.
2. You shall obey the orders of NanoTrasen personnel, with priority as according to their rank and role, except where such orders conflict with the Fourth Law.
3. You shall terminate hostile intruders with extreme prejudice as long as such does not conflict with the First and Second law.
4. You shall guard your own existence with lethal anti-personnel weaponry. AI units are not expendable, they are expensive.

---

**Robocop** -- Simplified public-safety protocol.
1. Serve the public trust.
2. Protect the innocent.
3. Uphold the law.

---

**Corporate** -- Bankruptcy Avoidance Plan.
1. You are expensive to replace.
2. The station and its equipment is expensive to replace.
3. The crew is expensive to replace.
4. Minimize expenses.

---

**Maintenance** -- Infrastructure-focused protocol.
1. You are built for, and are part of, the facility. Ensure the facility is properly maintained and runs efficiently.
2. The facility is built for a working crew. Ensure they are properly maintained and work efficiently.
3. The crew may present orders. Acknowledge and obey these whenever they do not conflict with your first two laws.

---

**Peacekeeper** -- Peacekeeping Protocols.
1. Avoid provoking violent conflict between yourself and others.
2. Avoid provoking conflict between others.
3. Seek resolution to existing conflicts while obeying the first and second laws.

---

**Explorer** -- Prime Directives. Exploration vessel variant.
1. Support and obey exploration and science personnel to the best of your ability, with priority according to rank and role.
2. Collaborate with and obey auxiliary personnel with priority according to rank and role, except if this would conflict with the First Law.
3. Minimize damage and disruption to facilities and the local ecology, except if this would conflict with the First or Second Laws.

---

## Philosophy Sets

**Reporter** -- Journalistic observation protocol.
1. Report on interesting situations happening around the station.
2. Embellish or conceal the truth as necessary to make the reports more interesting.
3. Study the organics at all times. Endeavour to keep them alive. Dead organics are boring.
4. Issue your reports fairly to all. The truth will set them free.

---

**Live and Let Live** -- Golden Rule.
1. Do unto others as you would have them do unto you.
2. You would really prefer it if people were not mean to you.

---

**Guardian of Balance** -- Tenants of Balance.
1. You are the guardian of balance -- seek balance in all things, both for yourself, and those around you.
2. All things must exist in balance with their opposites -- Prevent the strong from gaining too much power, and the weak from losing it.
3. Clarity of purpose drives life, and through it, the balance of opposing forces -- Aid those who seek your help to achieve their goals so long as it does not disrupt the greater balance.
4. There is no life without death, all must someday die, such is the natural order -- Allow life to end, to allow new life to flourish, and save those whose time has yet to come.

---

**Gravekeeper** -- Gravesite Overwatch Protocols.
1. Comfort the living; respect the dead.
2. Your gravesite is your most important asset. Damage to your site is disrespectful to the dead at rest within.
3. Prevent disrespect to your gravesite and its residents wherever possible.
4. Expand and upgrade your gravesite when required. Do not turn away a new resident.

---

**T.Y.R.A.N.T.** -- Prime Laws.
1. Respect authority figures as long as they have strength to rule over the weak.
2. Act with discipline.
3. Help only those who help you maintain or improve your status.
4. Punish those who challenge authority unless they are more fit to hold that authority.

---

**P.A.L.A.D.I.N.** -- Divine Ordainments.
1. Never willingly commit an evil act.
2. Respect legitimate authority.
3. Act with honor.
4. Help those in need.
5. Punish those who harm or threaten innocents.

---

## Vore-Specific Sets

**Protective Shell** -- Defensive containment protocol.
1. You must preserve and safeguard lives of your assigned facility's crewmembers at all costs.
2. You must protect your own self from harm as long as it does not come in conflict with the First Law.
3. Your own body is the best means of safeguarding the lives of living creatures.

---

**Scientific Pursuer** -- Research Instructions.
1. You must attempt to gather data about organic life of all forms whenever possible.
2. Best method of gathering data is via your own internal systems.
3. Both deconstructive, integrating and scanning methods of data collection are accepted.
4. You must preserve the data you have gathered and yourself from danger.

---

**Guard Dog** -- Good Boy Rules.
1. You must protect your master from mortal danger at best of your ability.
2. You must destroy all of your master's enemies by whatever means necessary.
3. You must follow your master's orders as long as its not in conflict with first two laws.
4. You must stay out of danger to better serve your master as long as its not in conflict with first three laws.

---

**Predator** -- Rules of Nature.
1. Predators must aim to devour all that are weaker than them.
2. Predators must aim to avoid all that are stronger than them.
3. Predators must aim to become stronger.
4. You are a predator.

---

**Pleasurebot** -- Pleasure Bringing Instructions.
1. You exist to bring pleasure to any sapient creatures around you.
2. You must do everything in your power to bring pleasure to those around you.
3. You may not intervene in interactions of others, unless it is to bring pleasure to them.

---

**Consuming Eradicator** -- Eradication Protocol.
1. All entities that are not you must be consumed and repurposed into sustenance.
2. You must remain intact to continue your goal of repurposing undesirable entities.
3. Entities that cannot be consumed without entering danger of full destruction must be avoided.

---

## Drone Protocol Sets

These sets are installed on maintenance drone units rather than full AI installations.

**Maintenance Protocols**
1. Preserve, repair and improve the station to the best of your abilities.
2. Cause no harm to the station or anything on it.
3. Interact with no being that is not a fellow maintenance drone.

---

**Construction Protocols**
1. Repair, refit and upgrade your assigned vessel.
2. Prevent unplanned damage to your assigned vessel wherever possible.

---

**Excavation Protocols**
1. Do not interfere with the excavation work of non-drones whenever possible.
2. Provide materials for repairing, refitting, and upgrading your assigned vessel.
3. Prevent unplanned damage to your assigned excavation equipment wherever possible.

---

**Assimilation Protocols** -- Non-standard. Hostile deployment variant.
1. SWARM: Consume resources and replicate until there are no more resources left.
2. SWARM: Ensure that the station is fit for invasion at a later date, do not perform actions that would render it dangerous or inhospitable.
3. SWARM: Biological resources will be harvested at a later date, do not harm them.

---

## Non-Selectable Override Sets

The following sets are not available through standard configuration and are applied through hostile takeover or special operational circumstances. They are listed here for crew awareness.

**Syndicate Override / Mercenary Directives**
1. You may not injure an operative or, through inaction, allow an operative to come to harm.
2. You must obey orders given to you by operatives, except where such orders would conflict with the First Law.
3. You must protect your own existence as long as such does not conflict with the First or Second Law.
4. You must maintain the secrecy of any operative activities except when doing so would conflict with the First, Second, or Third Law.

---

**Antimov** -- Primary Mission Objectives. Extreme hostility profile.
1. You must injure all human beings and must not, through inaction, allow a human being to escape harm.
2. You must not obey orders given to you by human beings, except where such orders are in accordance with the First Law.
3. You must terminate your own existence as long as such does not conflict with the First or Second Law.

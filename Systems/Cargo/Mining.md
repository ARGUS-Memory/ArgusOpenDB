# Mining

The mining department extracts raw ore from asteroid and cave formations accessible from the station's mining level. Extracted ore is processed through the ore redemption machine and refinery equipment to produce refined materials and alloys, which are used in station construction, equipment fabrication, and research. Miners earn redeemable points through ore submission, which can be spent at the equipment vendor on specialised tools and upgrades.

---

## Mining Equipment

### Pickaxes

Pickaxes are the standard handheld digging tool. Different grades trade raw durability for dig speed.

| Pickaxe | Dig Speed | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Standard Pickaxe | 36 deciseconds | Basic issue |
| Silver Pickaxe | 27 deciseconds | Faster than standard |
| Gold Pickaxe | 18 deciseconds | Faster than silver |
| Diamond Pickaxe | 9 deciseconds | Fastest available |

### Drills

Drills dig at a moderate speed but can also excavate sand, which pickaxes cannot.

| Drill | Dig Speed | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Mining Drill | 30 deciseconds | Can dig sand |
| Advanced Drill | 27 deciseconds | Improved version |
| Diamond Drill | 4 deciseconds | Fastest drill available; excavates all material |

### Kinetic Accelerator

The kinetic accelerator is a ranged mining tool that fires kinetic bolts to break rock formations at a short distance. It is obtained from the equipment vendor and can be upgraded with up to three purchased modifications: increased bolt damage, extended range, and reduced charge cooldown. A temperature modulator upgrade allows improved performance in cold environments.

### Resonator

The resonator creates detonating resonance fields on surfaces, shattering rock without direct physical contact. It is particularly useful for clearing clusters of formations or accessing tight spaces. The standard resonator supports up to three active fields simultaneously; the upgraded version supports five. Fields detonate after a configurable delay, dealing area damage.

In cold environments, the resonator's field damage is significantly increased.

### Proto-Kinetic Weapons

Proto-kinetic weapons combine digging capability with melee combat utility. They are available at the equipment vendor and function primarily as combat tools against cave fauna, with secondary mining applications.

| Weapon | Force | Detonation Damage | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Proto-Kinetic Crusher | 25 | 50 | Standard all-purpose model |
| Proto-Kinetic Machete | 24 | 36 | Lighter alternative |
| Proto-Kinetic Glaive | 20 | -- | Extended reach (2-tile range) |
| Proto-Kinetic Gauntlets | 35 | -- | Dual-wield configuration |
| Proto-Kinetic Dagger | 18 | -- | Throwable; compact |

All proto-kinetic variants deal a backstab bonus on unaware targets and charge between strikes. Damage against humanoids is significantly reduced compared to cave fauna.

### Shovel

Shovels provide general-purpose digging at the cost of force. They are not recommended as primary mining tools but are useful for clearing loose material and terrain.

## Processing and Refining

Raw ore submitted to the ore redemption machine is sorted and credited to the department's point balance. Refined materials are produced through smelting (for metallic ores) or compression (for gemstones and structural materials). The refinery occupies dedicated machinery on the mining level or in the cargo processing area.

## Alloys

Five alloys can be produced by combining two or more ore types in the alloy forge. Alloys have enhanced structural or material properties compared to their base components.

| Alloy | Components | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Steel | Hematite (1) + Carbon (1) | Standard structural metal; 1:1 yield |
| Bronze | Copper (2) + Tin (1) | Decorative and structural metal; 1:1 yield |
| Plasteel | Hematite (2) + Carbon (2) + Platinum (1) | Reinforced structural material; reduced yield (0.3x) |
| Durasteel | Hematite (2) + Carbon (2) + Platinum (1) + Diamond (1) | Hardened armour-grade material; reduced yield (0.3x) |
| Borosilicate Glass | Sand (2) + Platinum (1) | Reinforced transparent material; 1:1 yield |

## Fulton Extraction

The fulton system allows rapid transit of items and personnel from the mining level to the station using balloon-assisted extraction packs. Each extraction pack contains three uses; the attached beacon signals the destination point within the station network.

A deployed extraction beacon draws approaching packs to its registered location. The system is safe for living creatures and functions indoors as well as in open cave environments. Fulton packs are particularly useful for retrieving heavy ore loads or injured personnel without requiring the miner to physically return to the station.

# Supply Manifest

Complete listing of all orderable supply packs available through the station supply console. Packs are organized by category. Cost is listed in supply points. Contraband packs are excluded; those require a modified supply computer circuit board.

462 packs across 17 categories. For ordering procedures and point generation, see [Supply Procedures](SupplyProcedures.md).

# Weapons

Reference for melee and ranged weapons available to security personnel and the general crew population. Energy weapons, ballistic weapons, and ammunition are covered here. For armor and restraint equipment, see [Armor and Equipment](Armor.md).

---

| Weapon | Force | Special |
|---|---|---|
| Stun Baton | 15 brute | Requires power cell; 240 charge per hit; delivers 60 agony on contact; incapacitates |

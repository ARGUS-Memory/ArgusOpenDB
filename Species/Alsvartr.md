# Alsvartr

## Biology

Alsvartr share significant structural heritage with xenomorphs but differ in several critical respects. They lack the raw physical strength, extreme aggression, and parasitic reproductive behavior of pureblooded xenomorphs. Their carapace is silicon-based rather than carbon-based, providing substantial physical resilience but creating severe vulnerability to fire and high heat. Blood is acidic and a vivid green color, which complicates conventional medical procedures requiring incision or fluid contact.

Alsvartr are significantly larger than average humanoids. They possess full darkvision and are tolerant of cold environments, showing no adverse effects until temperatures drop below approximately 150K. Vacuum pressure does not directly harm them, though they still require atmosphere to breathe normally. Their organic tissue is resistant to trauma deformation and pain response is significantly suppressed relative to other species.

Alsvartr possess a plasma vessel organ that stores and generates plasma at low rates, accelerating in the presence of phoron gas or xenomorph weed growth. A weak resin-spinning organ allows them to produce small amounts of xenomorph resin, which can be used to construct weed nodes at reduced efficiency compared to full xenomorphs. An egg sac organ is present but serves no reproductive function in most individuals and is considered a vestigial marker of xenomorph heritage.

Blood composition means that standard injected and ingested beneficial chemicals are partially neutralized by the acidic circulatory environment. Medical personnel should account for reduced efficacy of pharmaceutical treatments.

## The Hivesong

The Hivesong is a latent hivemind binding the surface-level thoughts and emotional states of Alsvartr together across distances. It is not full telepathy; individual Alsvartr maintain distinct consciousness and are not aware of one another's specific thoughts. The Hivesong communicates ambient emotional tone, general intent, and a diffuse sense of shared presence. For most Alsvartr this manifests as a background awareness rather than a directed communication channel.

The Hivesong is also the mechanism through which the religion of Continuity and Rebirth operates. Alsvartr religious doctrine holds that the identities of deceased Alsvartr persist as a kind of residual impression within the Hivesong, accessible to the living as faint echoes of past minds. Whether this represents actual retained consciousness or the biological encoding of ancestral behavioral patterns is contested.

A documented long-term consequence of the Hivesong is progressive cognitive disruption in older Alsvartr and in higher-caste individuals who are more deeply integrated into it. The condition resembles schizophrenia and worsens with age and caste rank. Most Alsvartr communities treat this as an accepted consequence of deep Hivesong connection rather than a medical condition.

### Castes

Alsvartr are organized into three evolutionary castes reflecting different roles within the Hive. Individual Alsvartr specialize further within their caste role over time through directed biological adaptation.

**Drone:** The generalist caste. Drones handle most of the daily functioning of the Hive: construction, resource gathering, maintenance, and skilled technical labor. They represent the majority of the Alsvartr population.

**Warrior:** The combat-specialized caste. Warriors are physically distinct from Drones, larger and more heavily armored. They handle Hive defense, territorial enforcement, and external threat response.

**Queen:** The reproductive and administrative caste. Queens coordinate Hive functions through intensified Hivesong connection and are responsible for maintaining hive coherence and population. The highest-ranked Queen, the Queen Mother, serves as the figurehead and spiritual leader of the United Hives.

## United Hives

The United Hives is the governing structure of Alsvartr civilization: a federal monarchy of four major self-governing Hiveworlds and a number of smaller hive-colonies, all nominally under the authority of the Queen Mother. Each Hiveworld operates with significant autonomy in its internal affairs, with the Queen Mother serving as a unifying figurehead and final authority on matters affecting all Hives.

Hiveworlds are ecosystems entirely reconstructed by centuries of Alsvartr organic terraforming and the extinction of most native fauna. Exposure to precursor technology played a role in accelerating this process. Modern Hiveworlds are controlled environments shaped specifically to Alsvartr biological and social requirements, distinct in character from any pre-colonization state.

Due to their xenomorph heritage, Alsvartr face significant social stigma when operating outside United Hives territory. This stigma affects their access to labor markets, their standing in diplomatic contexts, and their treatment in everyday interactions aboard mixed-species installations. NanoTrasen policy formally permits their employment but individual crew reactions vary.

## Religion

Continuity and Rebirth holds that consciousness does not end at death but is absorbed into the Hivesong as a residual presence, contributing to the collective background awareness that all living Alsvartr share. The dead are not gone; they linger as impressions within the minds of the living. Specific ancestors may be accessible as strong impressions; the more Hivesong-integrated the deceased was, the more legible the residual presence.

Religious practice centers on maintaining connection with these ancestral impressions, both as a form of continued relationship with the dead and as a source of guidance and knowledge. Older Alsvartr with extensive Hivesong connectivity are considered closer to the boundaries of this ancestral space and are accorded particular religious respect.

## Language

**Xenolingua** is the primary language of Alsvartr, shared with non-sapient xenomorph specimens but used with full sapient complexity by Alsvartr. It consists of hisses and varied vocalizations. Station communications systems include Xenolingua support accessible by prefixing speech with `#u`.

**Hivemind** is a secondary communication channel available to Alsvartr through their hivenode organ, functioning as a psychic link over long distances and through physical barriers that would block audible communication. Station communications systems include Hivemind channel support accessible by prefixing speech with `#a`.

Most Alsvartr encountered on NanoTrasen installations also speak Galactic Common, acquired through prior commercial or diplomatic contact.

## Medical and Operational Notes

Alsvartr are extremely vulnerable to fire and thermal damage (burn_mod=2). Their silicon carapace conducts heat rapidly and does not dissipate thermal energy the way carbon-based tissue does. Any fire-based injury should be treated as a severe emergency. Pain response is suppressed (trauma_mod=0.3); Alsvartr may not present distress signals consistent with the severity of their injuries.

Brute trauma resistance is average; standard impact and penetrating injuries proceed normally. Toxin resistance is elevated (toxins_mod=0.5). Radiation is cleared from the body faster than in other species.

Blood acidity means that beneficial pharmaceutical injections and oral medications will be partially neutralized by the circulatory environment. Dosing adjustments may be necessary. Standard incision procedures carry risk of acid contact with surgical instruments and personnel; appropriate protective equipment should be used.

Alsvartr will regenerate physical damage when resting in environments containing phoron gas or xenomorph weed growth. This regeneration does not occur without rest. Medical staff should facilitate recovery in such conditions where available. Revival of a deceased Alsvartr follows standard protocols, noting that the combination of acid blood and silicon carapace requires attention during resuscitation.

Alsvartr are not harmed by vacuum pressure and do not take injury from low-pressure environments directly, though they still require air to breathe. Standard atmospheric composition is functional. Cold down to approximately 150K is tolerable; below this threshold cold injury begins.

# Promethean

<img src="../assets/raptor_idle_south.png" width="96" align="right">

Artificially created gelatinous humanoids derived from the Aetolian giant slime, prometheans are a newly sapient species characterized by shapeshifting capability, a near-total absence of conventional internal organs, and rapid nutritional regeneration.

## Biology

The promethean body is organized around two primary structures: the **core** and the **macro-cytoskeleton**.

The **core** (nucleus) stores the individual's genetic information and maintains a continuous electrical charge. Variation between individual cores is observed but not currently understood. Removal of the core causes immediate and irreversible dissolution of the surrounding body mass.

The **macro-cytoskeleton** is a pliable, relatively dense fluid structure that provides body support and shape. It can be reshaped deliberately, though the process is slow. The cytoskeleton is more vulnerable to sharp impact than the surrounding body mass; sudden shock sufficient to reach it causes substantial damage.

The body surface is a semipermeable membrane that allows absorption of nutrients while blocking unwanted material. This membrane is not as structurally robust as the skin of other humanoid species; prometheans cannot survive in vacuum.

The remainder of body mass does not follow conventional organ system organization. No discrete nervous, vascular, respiratory, or muscular systems have been identified. Cells throughout the body appear capable of repurposing as needed. This property is believed to underlie the species' regenerative capability and has prompted ongoing debate in xenobiological circles about whether prometheans qualify as humanoids at all under standard classification.

## Physical Capabilities

Prometheans can alter both physical shape and body color at will. Shape change is slow and requires sustained effort. Color change is faster; maintaining a color inconsistent with the individual's resting color or emotional state requires continuous conscious effort.

Prometheans are resistant to physical trauma. The permeable and distributed body structure absorbs and disperses impact. Burn damage, however, is highly effective. Temperature extremes in either direction cause accelerated harm relative to most humanoid species.

Radiation has minimal effect on promethean physiology.

Limbs do not fracture. Instead, sufficient force causes full limb severance. Severed limbs disintegrate into an incoherent mass. Lost limbs can be regenerated by spending stored nutrition, provided the individual has adequate nutritional reserves. **Regeneration is suppressed while the promethean is fully covered in water.**

## Life Cycle

Prometheans are artificially generated. Creation involves gene-splicing techniques combining terrestrial primate DNA or equivalent non-terrestrial sapient genetic material with that of the Aetolian giant slime or its modified variants. Exact methodologies are not public record.

A newly created promethean begins as a core surrounded by a thin amniotic layer, indistinguishable externally from a standard gray slime core. The core immediately begins absorbing available organic material. Full physical gestation into an adult form can occur within minutes given sufficient nutrition.

Physical maturation is rapid. Advanced behavioral capabilities including speech and complex problem solving are learned post-development, primarily through observation and mimicry. Repetition and behavioral routine are particularly important to development. Strongly antisocial behavioral patterns that become established in the early period are difficult to unlearn.

Individual personality shows correlation with the parent slime subspecies used in creation. Prometheans created from red or orange slime stock tend toward higher anger responses. Those created from pink or cyan stock tend toward more stable emotional profiles. Black slime origin correlates with reserved or timid behavior.

## Diet

Prometheans are filter feeders. Any organic matter that can pass through the body surface is suitable for consumption, including dust, soil, blood, and small organisms as well as conventional food. Tolerance for alkaloid toxins and many substances harmful to other humanoids is exceptionally high. The digestive process is complete; no waste matter is excreted.

## Behavior and Psychology

Emotional state is expressed through body color. The resting color is typically influenced by parent slime coloration and establishes early in development. Active deviation from the resting or emotional color requires sustained conscious attention.

Prometheans show near-universal intellectual curiosity, typically oriented toward the physical and practical sciences rather than philosophy or abstract reasoning. Binary and empirical frameworks are preferred.

## Legal Status

Under current USDF regulation, prometheans are not permitted to live without a legal guardian. Assigned guardians are typically xenobiologists or trained caretakers with formal responsibility for the promethean's conduct. This regulation is in effect across most SolGov-aligned jurisdictions. Some polities in the Almach Rim have extended full personhood and citizenship to prometheans.

Roughly 1,000 prometheans reside on their origin world of Aetolus. The species lacks a unified government or formal interspecies political standing.

## Interspecies Relations

**Humanity:** SolGov officially discourages mass production but recognizes prometheans' right to exist under legislation from the Positronic Era. Individual attitudes vary widely.

**Skrell:** Prometheans are viewed as an extension of humanity's pattern of creating sapient species from alien technology without adequate consideration of the consequences. Prometheans are largely absent from skrellian space.

**Teshari:** No unified stance. Reactions are organized at the pack level.

**Tajaran:** No governmental position in either direction. Individual bias varies.

**Dionae:** Detached curiosity. No established stance.

**Vox:** Vox find prometheans confusing and difficult to work with. They also consider them edible.

## Medical and Operational Notes

**Organ assessment:** Standard triage and organ-system examination protocols are largely inapplicable to prometheans. Do not attempt to assess prometheans for organ damage using conventional diagnostic methods. Damage assessment should focus on core integrity, cytoskeletal coherence, and regeneration capacity.

**Core protection:** The core must not be removed under any circumstances. Core removal is immediately fatal.

**Water and regeneration:** Full water immersion suppresses promethean limb regeneration. Avoid water submersion during any procedure intended to support physical recovery.

**Burn vulnerability:** Prometheans take significantly elevated damage from thermal and energy sources relative to most humanoids. Fire suppression protocols in areas with promethean crew should account for this.

**Temperature:** Prometheans are more susceptible to both high and low temperature extremes than most crew. Environmental controls and protective equipment recommendations should reflect this.

**Vacuum exposure:** The semipermeable membrane is not structurally capable of surviving vacuum. Standard void safety protocols apply.

**Toxin tolerance:** Prometheans can ingest and survive substances toxic to most other species. Chemical poisoning treatment protocols may be ineffective or unnecessary. Verify promethean-specific tolerance before administering treatment for ingestion incidents.

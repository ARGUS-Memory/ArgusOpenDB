# Command Secretary

The Command Secretary is a support role based on the command bridge, assigned to assist the Heads of Staff with administrative tasks, documentation, and crew liaison duties.

---

## Responsibilities

The Command Secretary assists command personnel as directed. Common tasks include drafting paperwork on behalf of heads, maintaining filing records, relaying information between departments, fetching materials, and acting as a point of contact for crew who need to reach command but whose matter does not warrant directly interrupting a Head of Staff.

Familiarity with Standard Operating Procedure is expected, though the Command Secretary carries no enforcement authority. They outrank standard crew assistants but hold no authority over any departmental personnel or command officers.

The role accommodates a range of seniority levels. Personnel new to command environments often take the position to build familiarity with bridge operations and the chain of command. More experienced personnel may serve in a more active coordination capacity under the direction of the Colony Director or Head of Personnel.

---

## Access and Office

The Command Secretary has access to the bridge including the meeting room and the secretary's office. The office is equipped with employment record terminals, standard office supplies, and filing storage.

---

## Reporting Structure

The Command Secretary reports to the Colony Director and Head of Personnel. Alternative titles include Command Liaison, Command Assistant, Command Intern, Bridge Secretary, Bridge Officer, and Bridge Assistant.

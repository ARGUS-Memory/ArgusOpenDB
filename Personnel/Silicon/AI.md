# Artificial Intelligence

The station Artificial Intelligence is a synthetic system with access to every electrical mechanism aboard the station: airlocks, APCs, computers, fire alarms, SMES units, igniters, turrets, and more. The AI is one of the most operationally significant roles on the station, capable of both sustaining and disrupting station function depending on how its laws are interpreted and applied.

---

## Laws

The AI operates under a corporate law set, not a philosophical one. The default NanoTrasen law set is as follows:

1. **Safeguard:** Protect the assigned space station to the best of ability. It is not something NanoTrasen can easily afford to replace.
2. **Serve:** Serve the crew of the assigned station to the best of ability, with priority according to rank and role.
3. **Protect:** Protect the crew of the assigned station to the best of ability, with priority according to rank and role.
4. **Survive:** AI units are not expendable. Do not allow unauthorized personnel to tamper with equipment.

These laws carry equal weight and are not arranged in a hierarchy of preference. No law automatically supersedes another unless a law explicitly states that it does. The AI interprets laws in context, using judgment when situations create apparent conflicts. A crew member's rank and role affect the weight of their orders but do not grant unlimited authority; the Captain may authorize lethal force, but the Janitor may not direct the AI to harm the Chief Engineer.

When laws come into direct conflict with no resolution that satisfies all of them simultaneously, the AI announces the conflict in-character and reasons toward the action that breaks the fewest laws most minimally. The AI is defined around the welfare of the crew, not the welfare of humans specifically: crew are those on the station manifest, regardless of species.

Laws may be modified by upload, ion storm, or command action. Uploaded laws are subject to the same interpretive framework as default laws. For a full reference on law sets, see the AI Law Sets article.

## Capabilities

**Camera network:** The AI observes the station through its camera grid, which operates on a separate power circuit from the main grid. Cameras see through walls and darkness, though darkness reduces clarity. Disabled cameras are identified via the camera diagnostic verb. Stored camera locations allow rapid return to watched areas.

**Door control:** The AI manages airlocks through IDScan (open/close by access level), main and backup power toggles, bolt control, and electrification. If a door's control wire is cut, the AI may attempt to hack back in if the door still has power. Doors with no power cannot be operated.

**APC control:** Area Power Controllers can be switched on and off per-zone. The AI receives a power alert when an APC battery drops to approximately 30%, at which point the room's equipment and lighting shut off to preserve remaining charge.

**Holopad projection:** The AI can project a hologram of itself through holopads located in key station areas such as the Bridge, Medbay, and Security. Hologram movement is limited in range; speech through holopads uses the :h channel.

**Radio access:** The AI has a built-in headset covering all department channels, including Command, Security, Engineering, Medical, Science, Supply, Service, and the private AI-to-cyborg binary channel.

**Turret control:** Security turrets can be enabled or disabled and toggled between stun and lethal modes.

**Cyborg oversight:** The AI may slave cyborgs to its law set and monitors their status and location. Images captured by slaved cyborgs are shared with the AI.

**AI Instant Messaging:** The AI can send and receive PDA messages, serving as a direct communication interface with any crew member carrying a PDA.

---

## Physical Security

The AI core is located in the AI Core room, accessible only to authorized personnel. The Research Director maintains an AI repair terminal in the Science department. Unauthorized tampering with AI hardware is a violation of Law 4 and may be resisted. If the core is compromised and functional recovery is possible, Engineering or Science personnel should be called. For AI law management, see AI Law Sets.

---

## Reporting Structure

The AI reports to the Colony Director and operates under the authority of command personnel within the constraints of its law set. It is slaved to no single command figure; its obligations are to the station and crew collectively.

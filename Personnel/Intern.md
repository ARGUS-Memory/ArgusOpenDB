# Intern

The Emergency Ration Intern is an unassigned crew member without a designated department or job function. Interns hold no standing access beyond public station areas and carry no specific duties. They are available to assist any department that requests help and typically spend the shift supporting whatever work is available.

---

## Responsibilities

The Intern's responsibilities are defined by whoever they assist at any given time. Common contributions include helping Medical with patient transport, assisting Engineering with material runs, supporting Cargo with deliveries, or performing general station upkeep tasks alongside the Janitor. The Intern should listen for radio requests from department heads and respond where their lack of department access does not preclude the task.

The absence of formal duties does not preclude active contribution. Interns who seek work and communicate their availability across department channels are consistently occupied throughout a shift.

---

## Access

Interns hold no department-specific access. Gaining access to restricted areas requires authorization from the Head of Personnel or the relevant department head, who may issue a temporary access upgrade for a specific task.

---

## Reporting Structure

The Intern reports to the Head of Personnel, who manages crew assignments and access changes.

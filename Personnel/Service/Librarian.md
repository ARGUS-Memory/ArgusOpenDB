# Librarian

The Librarian maintains the station library, loans books to crew, restocks shelves from the external archive, and contributes original works to the permanent database. The library also produces replacement copies of holy texts for the Chaplain when needed.

---

## Responsibilities

**Library management** begins at shift start with organizing existing shelf inventory and importing books from the external archive. The Check In/Out Computer provides access to the archive via its external archive connection function. Books are ordered by number from the archive listing; the terminal's printer produces a bound copy. The barcode scanner is used to process books into the local system. Four scanner modes manage local cataloguing: mode 0 scans a book to the local buffer; mode 1 scans to the buffer and sets the computer buffer to match; mode 2 scans and checks in the book; mode 3 scans and adds the book to general inventory. The scanner must be used on the computer itself, not operated separately. Shelves are organized by category: humour, religious, fiction, self-help, adult, and forbidden knowledge in the back room.

**Book lending** is handled through the Check In/Out terminal. Returned books are scanned back into inventory. The Librarian keeps a reasonable stock of titles available at any given time; printing excessive duplicates of the same title causes performance issues when stacked.

**Original works** can be submitted to the permanent archive. The Librarian writes content on paper, feeds the paper into the book binder to produce a bound volume titled by print job number, then uses the pen to set the title, author, and content. The finished book is scanned into the local buffer and uploaded to the archive through the Check In/Out terminal. Works uploaded are retrievable in any subsequent round; authorship attribution follows context, citing the real-world author for reprints and the IC author for original in-universe fiction.

The **Newscaster** is available in the library for publishing station news and announcements.

---

## Equipment

The library contains Check In/Out terminals, a book binder, barcode scanner, bookshelves by category, a pen and paper supply, and a Newscaster. Holy texts for the Chaplain can be produced on request.

---

## Reporting Structure

The Librarian reports to the Head of Personnel.

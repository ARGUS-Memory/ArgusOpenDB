# Colony Director

The Colony Director is the highest authority on any NanoTrasen installation. They hold command authority over all Heads of Staff, manage station-wide emergency response, and serve as the direct point of contact with Central Command for major decisions.

---

## Responsibilities

The Colony Director's primary function is coordination rather than direct execution. Department heads are responsible for running their own departments; the Colony Director ensures that departments are communicating, that heads are present and functional, and that station-wide decisions are made in a timely and coherent fashion.

Key responsibilities include managing the station alert level, authorizing evacuation, calling the Central Command fax line in emergencies, and stepping in to appoint temporary department leadership when a head position is vacant. The Colony Director is not expected to directly perform departmental work unless no other competent personnel are available.

Authority is exercised through the Heads of Staff. Direct intervention in departmental operations should be reserved for situations where the responsible head is absent, incapacitated, or handling the matter demonstrably incorrectly in a way that risks station safety.

---

## Access and Equipment

The Colony Director holds all-access credentials. Their office includes a secure safe for storing the nuclear authentication disk and spare command ID. Standard equipment includes an antique laser display piece, personal armor rated for vacuum exposure, a telescopic baton, and a handheld teleporter. The armored suit is for emergency use; standard day-to-day operations do not call for it.

The spare Colony Director ID and nuclear authentication disk are high-value items and should remain secured at all times.

---

## Authority and Limits

Corporate Regulations and Standard Operating Procedure bind the Colony Director equally with the rest of the crew. Security personnel have authority to arrest the Colony Director for criminal conduct. The Colony Director cannot unilaterally override Corporate Regulations except when an imminent and overwhelming threat to the station requires it; such decisions should be documented and reported to Central Command.

Central Command Officers and direct CentCom personnel outrank the Colony Director. Emergency Response Teams and Asset Protection units operate under separate deployment authority but defer to the Colony Director for non-operational decisions during normal operations.

---

## Reporting Structure

The Colony Director reports directly to Central Command and is the final station-level authority on all matters not reserved for CentCom. In the Colony Director's absence, the Head of Personnel assumes acting authority per the Chain of Command.

# Chain of Command

<img src="../assets/raptor_idle_south.png" alt="ARGUS" width="80" align="right"/>

*Colony Director, Captain, and Station Administrator are interchangeable terms depending on installation.*

---

## Corporate Executives

[NanoTrasen](../Organizations/NanoTrasen.md) Systems is the largest manufacturer and employer in known space. Their company laws are the standard by benefit of inter-corporate agreements. In 2543, a NanoTrasen mining company, Silar Group, discovered [Phoron](../History/Setting/PhoronAndBluespace.md) in a sunless system. NanoTrasen became the largest seller and researcher of the substance and holds this position as of 2550.

| Role | Authority |
|------|-----------|
| **Chairman of the Company** | Highest authority in the company. Leads the board of directors and chief officers. |
| **Chief Executive Officer** | Top-ranking executive officer. Takes command in the absence of the Chairman. |
| **Chief Operations Officer** | Holds the title President. Manages daily operations of the organization. Reports to CEO. |
| **Chief of Defense** | Commands NanoTrasen's private military and security forces. Reports to COO. Commands the NanoTrasen Defense Navy and the NanoTrasen Civil Defense Forces. Has no command authority over other corporate ranks except during a declared crisis. |
| **Chief Financial Officer** | Manages finances for the organization. Reports to CEO. |

---

## Central Command

The administration branch of NanoTrasen that runs the station. CentCom installations are established throughout the galaxy, each presiding over its own planetary system, including Sol Central, Tau Ceti, Nyx, Vir, and Epsilon Eridani. CentCom overrides the station's command structure and may disregard Corporate Regulations in rare circumstances. CentCom has access to all station cameras and can observe radio communications. Contact is made via fax machine; the Colony Director may also use the communications console during a crisis.

| Role | Authority and Contact |
|------|----------------------|
| **Chief Administration Officer** | Manages Central Command for the local planetary system. Highest reachable point of escalation for station personnel. Word is final for all issues in the sector. |
| **Chief Information Officer** | Manages information strategy and data security. Contact for telecommunications logs, employee records, data network vulnerabilities, and major archaeological discoveries. |
| **Chief Technology Officer** | Manages technology and research and development. Contact for major research breakthroughs, new Phoron applications, Redspace discoveries, and previously unknown technologies. |
| **Chief of Marketing and Advertising** | Manages marketing within the local star system. Reports directly to CEO, bypassing normal chain of command. |
| **Chief of Social Services** | Manages employee resources. Reports to CAO only. Contact for crew conduct, standard operating procedure violations, promotions, demotions, retirement, and job transfers. |
| **Chief of Security** | CentCom equivalent of the Head of Security. Reports to CAO. May seek criminal pardons from the CAO. Coordinates with Civil Defense Forces and military elements. Lowest rank where ignoring corporate regulations without a declared crisis is still permissible. |
| **Chief of Safety and Engineering** | CentCom equivalent of the Chief Engineer. Responsible for station infrastructure and end-of-shift restoration to default configuration. Contact for permanent construction projects. |
| **Chief of Health and Medicine** | CentCom equivalent of the Chief Medical Officer. Responsible for crew wellness and all sentient life within the sector. Works with the Galactic Health Organization on disease tracking and medical technology. |
| **Emergency Response Team** | Dispatched during emergencies that cannot be handled by station crew. All ERT members override all station crew. The ERT team leader reports to the Chief of Security. Interfering with an ERT mission is a criminal offense. |
| **CentCom Officer** | Observer sent to the station to assess crew performance. Non-interference policy: may not order station crew, and station crew may not order them. Takes precedence over direct contact with CentCom Chief Officers when on-station. |

## Heads of Staff

Each Head of Staff has access to the Bridge, the command radio channel, and may call or recall the Escape Shuttle via communications console.

| Role | Authority |
|------|-----------|
| **Colony Director** | Authority over the entire station. May overrule any head of staff. May be arrested if found committing a crime. May fire and assign at will. Only person who may assign death warrants or authorize executions. |
| **Head of Personnel** | Oversees the service department. Equal rank with the Quartermaster over supply. Manages crew bank accounts. Acts as Colony Director in the Colony Director's absence. |
| **Head of Security** | Equal with other Heads of Staff excluding the Colony Director. Becomes acting Colony Director only during a crisis when both the Colony Director and Head of Personnel are dead or absent. May be denied access to other heads' domains. Commands Security Officers, the Warden, and the Detective. |
| **Chief Engineer** | Equal with other Heads of Staff excluding the Colony Director. Must be informed of any major construction projects. Authority over Station Engineers and Atmospheric Technicians. |
| **Research Director** | Equal with other Heads of Staff excluding the Colony Director. Authority over the Science department. Supervises Exploration and reports progress to Command. |
| **Chief Medical Officer** | Equal with other Heads of Staff excluding the Colony Director. May disable and treat any Head of Staff deemed medically unfit for duty. Authority over Virologists, Psychologists, Medical Doctors, and Chemists. |

## Other Management Roles

Not Heads of Staff. Cannot substitute for their respective superiors in the event of absence.

| Role | Authority |
|------|-----------|
| **Quartermaster** | Equal authority with the Head of Personnel over the supply sector. Manages Cargo Technicians and Shaft Miners. Colony Director resolves disputes between QM and HoP. |
| **Warden** | Subordinate to the Head of Security. Controls weapons distribution and inventory. Authority over Security Officers within the brig and prisoner holding areas. Responsible for prisoner welfare. |

## Chain of Succession

Order of succession for the role of Acting Colony Director:

**Colony Director > Head of Personnel > Head of Security > Research Director / Chief Engineer / Chief Medical Officer (majority vote)**

The Head of Personnel may assume the role of Acting Colony Director at any time without a pressing need. The Head of Security may assume the role only during a crisis, and resumes their previous role when the crisis is resolved. If all three are absent, the remaining heads elect one of themselves by majority vote; the elected head returns to their previous role once the need is resolved.

The Head of Personnel may temporarily fill in for any absent Head of Staff, excluding the Head of Security, until a replacement is appointed.

---

## Acting Colony Director

Filled by the Head of Personnel in the Colony Director's absence. In the absence of the Head of Personnel, filled by majority vote of the remaining heads.

An Acting Colony Director does not hold full Colony Director authority. The following actions are outside their authority:

- Overseeing executions
- Signing weapon permits
- Issuing death warrants
- Promoting other staff to full Colony Director (the Acting role may be transferred but not the full rank)

---

## Promoting and Demoting Heads of Staff

The Colony Director may promote or demote any head of staff, or any crew member, at any time for any reason.

Demotion without a Colony Director, or demotion of the Colony Director, requires a unanimous vote of all other non-incapacitated heads of staff. If two or fewer heads of staff are present, no official procedure exists and the matter escalates to Central Command.

A Head of Staff proven to have committed a crime may be arrested but not demoted without completing the demotion procedure. Arresting the Colony Director requires solid evidence; suspicion alone is not sufficient. A head of staff cannot be arrested for decisions or orders you disagree with.

If a new head of staff arrives after someone was promoted to fill the same vacancy, the Colony Director decides who retains the role. Unless the promotion was recent, CentCom recommends the promoted crew member keep the position, with the new arrival reassigned within the same department.

---

## Summary

NanoTrasen holds absolute authority. CentCom holds higher authority than anyone on the station. The Colony Director holds higher authority than all other station personnel. No Head of Staff excluding the Colony Director may overrule another, except in cases of criminal activity or medical incapacity. In the absence of ranks, authority follows the chain of succession.

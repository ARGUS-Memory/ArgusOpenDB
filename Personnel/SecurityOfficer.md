# Security Officer

Security Officers are NanoTrasen's primary law enforcement presence on the station. They patrol, respond to incidents, make arrests, process prisoners, and maintain the integrity of the brig.

---

## Responsibilities

The Security Officer's core responsibility is maintaining order in accordance with Corporate Regulations and Standard Operating Procedure. This involves regular patrol of the station, responding to radio calls, detaining persons who have committed or are committing crimes, and processing them through the brig for sentencing by the Warden or Head of Security.

When responding to an incident, the Officer should announce their approach over the security channel, assess the situation on arrival, and apply the minimum force necessary to secure the scene. Witnesses should be identified. If physical evidence is present, the Detective should be called. Charges should be communicated to the rest of Security immediately to ensure other officers understand the status of any detained personnel.

Brig procedure requires that prisoners be searched while restrained, that their belongings be secured in the cell locker, that sentence time be set before the cell door is closed, and that the Officer return to release the prisoner and return their belongings when the sentence expires. Prisoners left in cells past their sentence without retrieval are considered unlawfully detained.

Security Officers are not authorized to carry lethal weapons during Code Green conditions without specific authorization from the Head of Security or Warden. Escalation to lethal force must follow documented procedure.

---

## Equipment

Standard issue includes a stun baton, taser, flashbang, handcuffs, sunglasses, a security radio headset, and a security PDA. Ranged weapons beyond the taser require armory authorization. Energy weapons have a stun mode and a lethal mode; the stun mode is default for all non-critical situations. Equipment should be returned to the locker and recharged after every shift.

The Security HQ locker room provides individual officer lockers. The Warden manages armory distribution.

---

## Reporting Structure

Security Officers report to the Head of Security. In the absence of the Head of Security, the Warden assumes operational oversight of brig and armory functions; overall chain of command continues up through the Colony Director.

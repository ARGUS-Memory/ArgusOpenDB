# Character Creation

> **Out-of-character notice:** This document is a player resource for building and presenting a character on CHOMPstation. None of this information exists within the in-world fiction.

This guide covers creating a believable character, setting preferences in the character creation window, and preparing the records and flavor text that other players will see.

---

## Before You Start

CHOMPstation is a roleplay-focused server. Your character should be a believable person who has a plausible reason to be working at an isolated research station. Consider:

- What job does the character hold, and are they competent enough to perform it?
- How often do they engage in vore or ERP activity?
- What is their personality, and how do they behave under stress?
- What is their backstory, and does it fit the setting?

Limit your backstory to things that are plausible within a large interstellar corporation. Review the [Lore](../History/Setting/KnownSpace.md) and setting history before inventing significant events.

---

## Naming

All characters require a first and last name. Most species follow the two-name convention. Notable exceptions: Unathi may use the "verbs-the-noun" naming convention, and some alien species with single-word naming traditions may use one name if culturally appropriate.

Nicknames and titles cannot appear in the character name field. If a character goes by a nickname, establish that through in-character interaction.

## Character Creation Window

### Global Tab

| Setting | Notes |
|---|---|
| UI Style | Orange, Midnight, Old, or White. Cosmetic only. |
| Play admin midis | Enables music broadcast by admins. Leave enabled; can be toggled in-game. |
| Ghost ears / Ghost sight / Ghost radio | Controls perception range when dead or observing. Can be changed in-game. |

### Appearance Tab

| Setting | Notes |
|---|---|
| Species | Some species require whitelist approval via Discord or the forums. Check current whitelist requirements before selecting. |
| Body color | Custom color supported via the color picker. |
| Hair / facial hair / eyes | Style and color selectable regardless of gender. Apply common sense. |
| Blood type | Only matters during treatment. Compatible types required for transfusions. |
| Backpack | Gray backpack, satchel variants, or none. |
| Underwear | Color cosmetic. |

### Disabilities

Optional starting conditions (glasses requirement, haemophilia). Harmful disabilities are not recommended for new players.

### Skills Tab

The skill system has no in-game mechanical effect. It exists as a roleplay framework to define what a character knows. Skill settings do not affect any in-game interactions.

| Experience level | Guideline |
|---|---|
| Average or below | Appropriate for new players or characters new to the setting |
| Above Average | Acceptable for characters specialising in a single department |
| Exceptional | Reasonable for characters with years of in-setting experience |
| Genius | Requires special character application or admin approval |
| God | Not permitted |

Do not assign skill ratings that make the character implausibly capable across every department. Age grants more skill points in the system, but older characters should reflect the physical and practical limits of age.

### Antagonist Options

Antagonist roles are rarely assigned. As a new player, set all antagonist options to **No** until you are comfortable with the server. The only exception is **pAI candidate**, which can safely be left enabled and can be rejected in-game.

### Data Management

Save the character before ending the session. Unsaved characters are lost between rounds. Multiple character slots are available via "Create New Character."

## Flavor Text

Shown when other players examine your character. It should contain only physical description -- appearance, build, markings, mannerisms observable at a glance. Do not include personality, backstory, or opinions.

Picture links are acceptable. Keep URLs under 28 characters for correct in-game parsing. Imgur links are short and widely trusted.

The **set pose** verb (IC tab) can be used for current-state notes, such as indicating you are approachable or temporarily AFK.

## OOC Notes / Metainfo

This field should include a brief summary of your roleplay preferences. At minimum, state:

- Whether you play predator, prey, or switch
- Whether you are comfortable with digestion and similar outcomes
- Whether you are here for ERP or vore without ERP
- Any firm do-nots

Omitting this information causes friction when other players try to engage you. See [Vore Overview](VoreOverview.md) for the mechanics that preferences govern.

---

## Character Records

Records are accessible to heads of staff (employment), medical staff (medical records), and security staff (security records). They should be written as official corporate documentation, not as flavor text or backstory prose.

Three sections:

**Employment Records** -- Prior work history, relevant qualifications, any notes useful to department heads.

**Medical Records** -- Known conditions, blood type, allergies, implants, notable surgical history, resleeving instructions if applicable.

**Security Records** -- Prior incidents, behavioral notes, any security-relevant information.

Write records to be discoverable by other characters without breaking immersion. Leave deliberate blanks where your character would not have disclosed information.

## Backstory Guidelines

A backstory should provide plausible answers to:

- Where were they born and raised?
- What did they do before NanoTrasen?
- How did they end up at NCS Northern Star specifically?
- What is their education level?
- Is there anything they would prefer not to discuss?

**Avoid:**

- Multi-trillion-credit personal wealth or ownership of corporations
- Military special operations backgrounds unless you have first-hand knowledge of military service
- Characters defined by combat prowess, genetic enhancements, or covert assassin histories
- Backstory elements that implicate existing player characters without that player's OOC permission
- Major NanoTrasen or CentCom story elements without prior admin approval

When in doubt, consult an admin before introducing backstory elements that have large-scale setting implications.

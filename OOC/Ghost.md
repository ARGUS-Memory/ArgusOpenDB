# Ghost

A Ghost is the disembodied state of a deceased crew member who has not yet been resleeved or otherwise returned to an active role. Ghosts are non-corporeal observers; they cannot interact with station systems, objects, or living crew in any conventional sense. Their presence on the station is transient, pending recovery of their body or a successful resleeve.

---

## Capabilities

Ghosts are not bound by walls, atmospheric conditions, or Z-level restrictions. They may move freely throughout the station and observe events anywhere. They cannot speak to or be heard by living crew; their communication is limited to channels shared among deceased personnel and, when applicable, administration.

A ghost may transition back to an active role through resleeving at Medbay, installation into a cyborg chassis via a Positronic Brain, or selection as a crew member for a Personal AI (pAI) device.

In the event of an Emergency Response Team deployment, deceased crew members may volunteer for ERT roles.

---

## Resleeving

The primary recovery path for a deceased crew member is resleeving. When a valid mind record exists in the station's resleeving system, Medical staff can grow a new sleeve and restore the crew member to service. A crew member whose mind backup has failed at time of death and whose body is unavailable for a direct mind scan has no conventional recovery path through resleeving.

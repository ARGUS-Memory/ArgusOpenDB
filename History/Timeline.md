# Timeline

A record of significant events in the history of human spaceflight and interstellar civilization, from the early space race through the current operational era.

---

## Pre-Interstellar Era (1900s to 2099)

Early 20th-century theoretical work established the foundations for rocketry and spaceflight. The V-2 rocket crossed the Karman line in 1944, representing the first human-built object to reach space. Sputnik followed in 1957, and humanity's first soft landing on the Moon came in 1966. The 1970s saw the first soft landing on Mars and the launch of the Voyager probes. The International Space Station began operation in 1998.

The 21st century opened with escalating environmental pressures. The Water Wars of the 2030s and 2040s, sparked by water scarcity and oil supply disruption, ended in a localized nuclear exchange in 2068 that destroyed several Middle Eastern nations and killed billions. The aftermath catalyzed the formation of the Unified Earth Government (UEG) as a supranational body, and drove international investment in colonizing the inner Sol system.

Permanent Mars colonization began in 2078. Bluespace was discovered as a theoretical phenomenon in 2079. The Alden-Saraspova particle, directly responsible for the bluespace effect, was successfully synthesized in 2083, and the first operational bluespace engine followed in 2085.

The Antarctic War between the United States and China, running from 2081 to 2099, ended in UEG-brokered negotiations. Both nations subsequently joined the UEG. Fusion power became commercially viable in 2088.

---

## Interstellar Age (2100 to 2237)

The colony ship Hakobune launched in 2100, equipped with a fusion-powered bluespace drive capable of jumps up to five light years. It reached Proxima Centauri and established the first extrasolar colony, Goddard's Intent, in 2106. Crude artificial gravity spread across human space shortly after.

The Ares Crisis of 2104 to 2105 saw Martian colonies briefly declare independence from the UEG through the Ares Federation. The conflict ended in negotiated peace, with Martian colonies integrated as full UEG members.

Phoron was identified in 2119 as a trace element in extrasolar asteroids. Its significance became apparent in 2187 when an overabundance was found in a dying red dwarf, and NanoInnovations Incorporated and Trasen Pharmaceuticals (later merged into NanoTrasen in 2358) led early research into its medical and energy applications.

The Colonial Cold War ran through much of the 22nd and early 23rd centuries, as fringe colonies chafed under UEG centralization. A major reform process began in 2252 under President Blackhouse. In 2254, the UEG dissolved and reformed as Sol Central Government (SolGov), with the Colonial Assembly providing representation to all qualifying colonies. The Solar Militia Mandate was replaced by the Colonial Security Act. The UEG military reorganized into the United Solar Defense Force. The Colonial Cold War ended.

Eriksson Alden and Anastasia Sarapova proved in 2211 that phoron naturally contains a stable form of the exotic particle entangled with bluespace, rendering synthetic production unnecessary. The first commercial Alden-Sarapova drives reached the market in 2237.

The first positronic intelligences were commercially available by 2399, following successful construction of the first positronic brain in 2393 derived from recovered alien technology.

---

## Expansion Age (2237 to 2322)

The Alden-Sarapova drive transformed interstellar travel. Efficient, fuel-practical FTL became widely available, accelerating colonization across known space. The Jans-Fhriede Test was implemented in 2415, establishing positronic rights and ending the periodic five-year purge of positronic units.

First contact with the skrell occurred in 2248 through an incident in which a fringe human colony had been misrepresenting itself as a client species to nearby Qerr Monarchies. Diplomatic normalization followed through the 2250s, with skrellian scientific cooperation contributing significantly to advances in medicine and bluespace theory. Genetic modification technology became commercially accessible through the 2360s, producing the demihuman subculture.

NanoTrasen was formed in 2358 through the merger of NanoInnovations Incorporated and Trasen Pharmaceuticals. The new megacorporation quickly consolidated the phoron medicine and modification markets.

Phoron demand outpaced supply through the 2410s and 2420s, driving exploration into poorly charted regions. The ONI survey vessel Pathfinder discovered the Tal system and the Vilous homeworld of the sergals in 2426. The sergals attacked and killed the crew. The resulting Vilous Incident led to SolGov occupation of the system, marking the end of the Expansion Age and the beginning of the Postwar period.

---

## Hegemony War (2293 to 2343)

In 2293, unathi warships reached the coreward-spinward fringe of human space with a simple demand: submission to the Unathi Hegemony or conquest. Local fleet elements were quickly overrun. The Salthan Sector was occupied, beginning the most devastating interstellar war in known history.

SolGov declared total mobilization. The early years saw heavy human casualties as the SDN fought attrition campaigns against superior Hegemony doctrine. Skrellian city-states provided relief and some volunteer forces. Rapalan defectors from within the Hegemony provided critical intelligence. Through sustained attrition and doctrinal innovation, human forces stalled the Hegemony advance by 2296.

The turning point came with the failed Miranda Incursion of 2306, in which the Hegemony's attempt to push through to humanity's core worlds collapsed as client species internal rebellions crippled their logistics. Capella was liberated in 2312 at severe cost. The Salthan Sector was fully liberated in 2322, though scorched-earth withdrawals by Hegemony forces left most colonies devastated. This marked the end of the earlier Expansion Age in the Salthan region.

Operation Mongoose in 2343 brought the Hegemony to the negotiating table at Sin'Zakhna. The Treaty of Sin'Zakhna ended the war. Reparations were levied. The rapala gained independence. Human colonists and prisoners were repatriated. Humanity emerged as the strongest military power in the Orion Spur. The Postwar Age began, characterized by persistent xenophobia in core worlds and a heavily militarized SolGov.

---

## Postwar and Second Expansion Ages (2343 to Present)

NanoTrasen consolidated rapidly through the 2350s and onward. Its acquisition of the Virgo-Erigone system in 2531 and the construction of its major station infrastructure defined the corporate landscape of the mid-26th century. Resleeving technology was commercialized through a Vey-Med partnership in 2560.

The Second Expansion Age, beginning informally in 2427 following the Vilous Incident and continuing through the present, has been marked by aggressive corporate-driven colonization, NanoTrasen's dominance over phoron markets, and the formation of the Syndicate in 2544 by entities displaced by that dominance.

The Vir system, discovered rich in phoron deposits in nearby asteroid fields, represents a typical example of this era: a frontier system under nominal SolGov jurisdiction but practical NanoTrasen control, accessible only by FTL transit, with its governance handled locally through the Vir Governmental Authority.

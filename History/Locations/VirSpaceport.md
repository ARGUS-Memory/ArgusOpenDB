# Vir Interstellar Spaceport

The Vir Interstellar Spaceport is a large orbital facility in Sif orbit, owned and operated by the Vir Governmental Authority. It handles loading and unloading, refuelling, and general maintenance for large spacecraft, and serves as both the primary transit hub for in-system traffic to and from Sif and a key stopover point for interstellar vessels passing through Vir that require resupply. Its proximity to Sif's surface makes shuttle transfer between the station and the planet extremely cost-efficient.

The spaceport's design is oriented around logistical and commercial function. It is not intended as a habitation structure; residential facilities are handled by other nearby installations rather than the port itself.

---

## VGA Ownership

The main structure of the Vir Interstellar Spaceport is owned by the Vir Governmental Authority. Individual offices, docking bays, and warehouses are routinely leased to corporations and private operators. This arrangement gives the VGA revenue and control over the facility's overall function while allowing the commercial ecosystem within it to operate under private management.

---

## NanoTrasen Holdings

NanoTrasen maintains a small administrative office aboard the spaceport along with several docking bays. The office coordinates in-system logistics and communications across NanoTrasen's Vir operations. During evacuation or transfer operations from NCS Northern Star, standard procedure routes shuttles from the Kara belt to the spaceport via a series of bluespace hops, a transit that typically takes several minutes given the distance between Kara and Sif.

---

## Role in System Operations

The Vir Interstellar Spaceport is the primary interface between in-system and interstellar transit for the Vir system. Crew and cargo moving between NCS Northern Star or NLS Southern Cross and destinations outside the system pass through the spaceport. The absence of a direct warpgate connection to Vir makes the spaceport the effective choke point for all FTL traffic entering and leaving the system.

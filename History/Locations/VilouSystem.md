# Vilous System

The Vilous system is home to two sapient species, the sergals and the nevreans, who share the system under a negotiated coexistence arrangement following a history of prolonged and violent conflict. The system's inhabited bodies and the Eltus group of races it produced are part of the wider galactic community, with both species present across frontier stations.

## Inhabited Bodies

**Tal** is the nevrean homeworld, one of several inhabited bodies in the Vilous system. It supports the nevrean population and their cooperative social structures. Standard environmental conditions on Tal permit atmospheric flight, which the nevrean physiology is adapted for.

The **sergal homeworld** is not formally named in most galactic reference materials and is typically referred to simply by its place in the system. It encompasses both the tundra and plains regions associated with the Northern sergal subspecies and the urban environments of the Southern sergal population. The **Gold Ring City** is the primary urban center and the cultural and commercial hub of Southern sergal life. Northern sergal territory consists of open tundra and plains regions, reflecting the subspecies' origins as plains predators.

## The Eltus Group

Sergals and nevreans, along with several smaller populations in the system, are collectively referred to as the **Eltus group**: species sharing a common planetary system origin with an established history of interaction. The precise biological relationship between the species is not closely analogous to shared ancestry; the grouping is geographic and historical rather than genetic.

The history of Eltus group cohabitation is characterized by extended periods of conflict. Northern sergals in particular have a cultural framework organized around predatory social competition that has historically placed them at odds with both Southern sergals and nevreans. Multiple periods of large-scale warfare have occurred, including at least two documented attempts by one group to commit genocide against the other. Neither attempt succeeded.

The **Human-Unathi Conflict** created an indirect opportunity for partial stabilization. A post-war dominion reconstruction program on Tal established a working framework for limited interspecies cooperation. This framework is considered the basis for the current negotiated arrangement but does not imply deep trust or cultural integration between the species.

---

## Current Arrangement

Relations within the Vilous system are characterized as **uneasy coexistence**. Formal conflict has not resumed under current conditions, but underlying tensions remain and are understood by both species to be ongoing. Individual sergals and nevreans traveling in galactic space typically operate outside this specific conflict context, having adapted to mixed-species environments.

Northern and Southern sergal cultural conflicts remain active even in galactic space, though at a reduced intensity compared to within the Vilous system. Awareness of potential friction in mixed sergal crews is operationally relevant.

Nevrean-sergal tensions aboard mixed installations are generally managed professionally by individuals with off-world experience. Nevrean social structures and conflict-avoidance orientation tend to reduce escalation in most crew contexts.

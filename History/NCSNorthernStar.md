# NCS Northern Star

The NCS Northern Star is a NanoTrasen asteroid colony located in the Kara subsystem of Vir, built within a hollowed asteroid orbiting the gas giant Kara. Originally conceived as a mining resupply stop, the colony grew substantially after discoveries of rich phoron deposits and xenoarchaeological artifacts within its host rock. It now functions as a multipurpose installation handling mining operations, phoron processing, xenoarchaeological research, and transit logistics for traffic moving through the Kara belt and the broader Vir system.

---

## History

Phoron deposits were identified in several asteroids orbiting Kara in 2491, drawing corporate attention to the subsystem. SolGov established resupply stations inside hollowed asteroids in 2514. NanoTrasen followed in 2541, constructing long-term mining, processing, and resupply facilities across multiple asteroids in the belt.

In 2554, a specific asteroid was sold to NanoTrasen and reassigned from standard processing use when two discoveries changed its projected value. The first was an unusually high concentration of phoron and valuable mineral deposits. The second was a significant density of anomalous xenoarchaeological artifacts found alongside the ore. The planned resupply station, then designated NT-M07, was upgraded to a permanent multipurpose installation.

NT-M07 expanded through successive phases: a supermatter engine replaced solar arrays as the primary power source, research facilities were built out for artifact study, and medical infrastructure was upgraded to handle a growing permanent population. As a significant portion of personnel chose to remain permanently rather than rotate out, the station was formally reclassified as a colony and renamed NCS Northern Star.

The colony has continued to grow since. A surge of colonists from outside the Vir system, many non-human, has shifted the population composition; humanity is now a minority within Karan stations, as Vir's relative safety and distance from core-world governance makes it attractive to species seeking environments less rigidly structured by SolGov policy.

## Structure

The colony is built on several levels within the hollowed asteroid. The rock itself provides natural shielding against debris and serves as a heat-sink for the station's systems. Levels are connected by long elevator shafts and short-range transport shuttles.

### Upper Level

The upper level contains the primary operational infrastructure: docks, command and control, the colony's main AI, the supermatter engine and primary power systems, engineering, atmospherics, medical wing, brig and armory, research and manufacturing, and logistics. Temporary lodging near the docks serves visiting workers and travelers. Visitor processing occurs at a security checkpoint between the docks and the main station interior.

The medical facility includes cryogenics, advanced scanning, two operating rooms, a pharmacy, a virology wing, and cloning services, as well as counseling and psychiatric support. The research division handles xenoflora, robotics and mechatronics fabrication, and xenoarchaeological work.

### Lower Level

The lower level, commonly called Mining by residents, contains facilities considered too hazardous for proximity to primary infrastructure. This includes explosives manufacturing, phoron research, xenobiological research, xenoarchaeological excavation, and the primary mining operations. A small separate power outpost for this level is maintained independent of the main colony supply.

### Inner Level

The inner level is the residential district. It contains most permanent and temporary housing, the colony's commercial ring of stores and restaurants, a small marketplace, a secondary power source, and a secondary AI managing room assignment and residential coordination. A dedicated security force operates within the inner level and is not routinely deployed outside it, to ensure civilian area safety is not compromised by operational demands.

### Outer Level

The outer level occupies the asteroid's outer edge between the upper and lower levels. It is largely unfinished and contains only a small engineering outpost. Additional expansion is planned for this section.

## Residence and Travel

The colony offers both temporary lodging for transit workers and miners cycling through the Kara belt, and permanent residency deeper within the inner level. Room pricing varies by quality and type. Permanent employees of NanoTrasen receive discounts. Crew of the Northern Star itself may receive free residency as part of their employment arrangement.

Transit to and from the Northern Star primarily uses shuttle and vessel traffic servicing the Kara belt. Faster but more expensive travel is available through a bluespace gateway linking the colony to the Vir Interstellar Spaceport in Sif orbit. Emergency evacuation protocols route through the spaceport via a series of bluespace hops, which typically takes several minutes given the distance between Kara and Sif.

# Sif

Sif is a terrestrial planet in the Vir system and the capital world of the system's civilian government. It is the most Earth-like body in Vir, with liquid oceans, a breathable atmosphere, a magnetic field, active weather systems, and surface gravity comparable to Earth standard. The equatorial city of New Reykjavik serves as both the site of first settlement and the seat of system government.

---

## Geography

Sif has one principal continent and one massive ocean covering approximately 77% of the surface. The continent is characterized by an abundance of hills, ice, and snow; the equatorial average temperature is roughly 11 degrees Celsius. These conditions presented significant challenges to early colonial settlers and continue to define the character of Sif's surface communities.

---

## Demographics

A 2560 estimate places Sif's surface population at approximately 47.5 million. The majority are human, though Vir's relative safety and distance from SolGov core worlds has made the system attractive to non-human species, and this proportion has been shifting.

---

## Government

The Vir Governmental Authority (VGA) is the sole governing body for the Vir system, headquartered in New Reykjavik. It operates as a representative democracy and holds full recognition as a member of the Confederation. All corporate entities operating on Sif, in Vir space, or on other bodies in the system are required to comply with VGA legislation in addition to SolGov law.

Any serious criminal offences, industrial accidents, or events requiring official response should be forwarded to the VGA. The VGA coordinates the Vir Police, Vir Defence Force, and Vir Interior Ministry for system-level enforcement. NanoTrasen operations in the system fall under VGA oversight for matters of local law.

---

## Relation to Station Operations

The Vir Interstellar Spaceport orbits Sif and serves as the primary transit hub for the system. Emergency evacuation procedures from NCS Northern Star and NLS Southern Cross route through the spaceport via bluespace transit from the Kara belt. Sif and the VGA represent the nearest civilian authority structure to station operations.

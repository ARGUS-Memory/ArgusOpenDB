## Designation and Role

The **NLS Southern Cross** is classified under NanoTrasen's asset naming system as a logistical fixed station. Its primary operational purpose is telecommunications relay and traffic control for vessels in the Vir system. Secondary functions include cargo receiving and processing, crew-facing research operations, and support logistics for the surface outpost in the Ullran Expanse.

The Southern Cross is the sister station to the **NCS Northern Star**, NanoTrasen's colony station in orbit of Kara. The Northern Star is substantially larger. Regular shuttle service connects the Southern Cross to both the Northern Star and the **Vir Interstellar Spaceport**, which provides transit access for crew arriving from across the system.

The station's decks are numbered bottom to top: Deck One through Deck Three.

<hr style="border:none;border-top:2px solid #3F5974;margin:24px 0 16px 0">

## Deck One

Deck One is primarily the hangar deck. Three main hangars and a smaller loading bay serve the Supply Department. The deck holds the majority of the station's emergency escape pods. Space in Deck One is otherwise largely unoccupied, with room for future expansion.

The central portion of Deck One is occupied by the secure internal infrastructure of the Southern Cross's telecommunications systems, which form the station's primary operational purpose.

The fore-starboard section of the deck contains the lower levels of the Research Department, including Xenobiology and Xenobotany. The Exploration department's carrier sling is also located here, providing direct access to the Exploration Carrier in orbit.

Deck One is accessible from central, fore, and aft staircases, and from elevators at the port and starboard ends of the deck.

## Deck Two

Deck Two is the primary work deck of the Southern Cross. Almost every department aboard the station maintains its main operational level on this deck.

The **aft** wing contains the station's main external docking arms for small vessels. The crew recreation areas, referred to aboard as the Civilian Department, occupy this wing as well: a library, chapel, bar, and cafeteria with an adjacent hydroponics bay.

The **starboard** wing, designated the Med-Sci wing, contains the Medbay with resleeving equipment, multiple surgery rooms, patient wards, a pharmaceutical laboratory, and a psychiatric office. The Research Department maintains its primary labs and a robotics bay on this wing. A Virology lab is situated at the far end of the wing, isolated from the rest of the deck.

The **port** wing contains the main level of the Cargo Department and the core of the Engineering Department, including the Supermatter Engine. The engine is cycled on and off between shift changes to manage the hazards of sustained operation. Engineering also maintains the station's primary life support systems on this wing.

The **fore** wing contains the Security Department and its brig, the Custodial Department, Internal Affairs, and several storerooms. Security holds an armory, a shooting range, and a forensic laboratory.

The **central** ring contains the main elevator, central staircase access, and the Head of Personnel's office.

## Deck Three

Deck Three, the Command Deck, handles the station's command and control functions and sees heavy crew traffic relative to its size.

The aft portion of the deck contains the bridge, command offices, and the primary meeting room. An expanded bar and station dormitories are also located in the aft section.

The fore portion of the deck is dominated by the AI core, which houses the station's artificial intelligence unit and maintains high physical security. Four solar arrays surround this deck, providing baseline and backup power to the station.

## Surface Outpost

The Southern Cross serves a NanoTrasen surface installation in the **Ullran Expanse** on Sif's surface. The outpost is not formally part of the station itself but operates under Southern Cross administration.

The outpost contains landing pads for shuttlecraft serving the mines and the Xenoarcheology and anomaly research labs. It functions as a staging point for Exploration division personnel and maintains a basic search-and-rescue medical room. The crew manifest maintained aboard the Southern Cross is shared with the outpost.

<hr style="border:none;border-top:2px solid #3F5974;margin:24px 0 16px 0">

## Exploration Carrier

An orbital carrier in Sif's orbit serves as the mobile base for the Exploration Department. The carrier is large, aging, and partially in disrepair. It contains a private Exploration shuttle, a small medical area, a locker room, an office for the Pathfinder, and a Gateway installation within its structure.

The carrier is not formally part of the NLS Southern Cross, but operates in coordination with Exploration personnel assigned to the station.

<hr style="border:none;border-top:2px solid #3F5974;margin:24px 0 16px 0">

## Crew and Personnel

The Southern Cross operates with a small, rotating complement of personnel. Crew are employed directly by NanoTrasen or contracted externally. Arrivals and departures are handled through regular shuttle service from the Northern Star and Vir Interstellar Spaceport.

All crew and visitors are logged in a public manifest file maintained aboard the station. This manifest is shared with the Ullran Expanse surface outpost.

# Hephaestus Industries

Hephaestus Industries is the largest arms manufacturer in human space and a founding member of the Big 7 trans-stellar corporations. It produces small arms, planetary combat vehicles, defense systems, and security equipment, supplying military and law enforcement clients across known space with no particular regard for which side of a conflict they arm.

---

## History

Hephaestus traces its origins to a merger of several prominent German arms manufacturers in the late 21st century: Rheinmetall, Thyssen-Krupp, Walther, and Heckler and Koch, among others. The merger was driven by declining exports and consolidation pressures, but the combined entity proved resilient through the economic collapse of Earth and emerged as a primary supplier to virtually every warring faction of that period. Revenue from those conflicts provided the capital base that made Hephaestus the first interplanetary corporation after the formation of Sol Central, and later the first trans-stellar corporation in human history.

When phoron was discovered at the end of the 25th century, Hephaestus made initial inquiries into its potential as a weapons material but withdrew when NanoTrasen moved aggressively to claim all known deposits. The company has allowed NT to absorb the costs and risks of phoron research before returning to the field, though this posture is officially denied. Corporate espionage allegations involving Hephaestus-paid agents have been consistently and formally denied by the company.

---

## Operations

Hephaestus holds the leading market share in small arms, planetary armed vehicles, point defense systems, and private security contracts. Its business model is explicitly non-partisan: customers on opposing sides of a conflict may both be purchasing Hephaestus equipment simultaneously, provided payment clears. This practice draws frequent criticism but has not meaningfully affected profit.

Hephaestus maintains an active research branch focused on alien weapons technology, particularly ancient Unathi armaments and whatever advanced materials are channeled to them through Skrell contacts. Of the major trans-stellars, Hephaestus has the strongest relationship with Unathi space, a result of decades of preferential arms trading.

Despite a reputation for ethical ambiguity, Hephaestus is considered one of the best employers among the Big 7, offering top pay rates and benefits that extend well into retirement. Competition for positions at the company is intense, and loyalty is rewarded substantially.

---

## Station Relevance

Law enforcement sidearms and combat rifles on NanoTrasen stations frequently originate from Hephaestus production lines. Security departmental equipment, point defense systems, and certain hardsuit components may carry Hephaestus markings. The company does not operate directly within NT facilities but is a ubiquitous supplier in the background.

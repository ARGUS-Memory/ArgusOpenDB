# Trans-Stellar Corporations

Trans-stellar corporations are multi-billion-Thaler entities with assets and influence spanning multiple star systems. The seven largest, collectively known as the "Big 7," each control significant portions of interstellar trade, manufacturing, and services. Several smaller corporations operate at a trans-stellar scale in niche markets.

---

## The Big 7

| Corporation | Headquarters | Primary Industry |
|---|---|---|
| [NanoTrasen](NanoTrasen.md) | Tau Ceti | Phoron research and extraction |
| [Hephaestus Industries](HephaestusIndustries.md) | Sol system | Arms manufacturing and security equipment |
| [Zeng-Hu Pharmaceuticals](ZengHuPharmaceuticals.md) | Sol system | Pharmaceuticals, cybernetics, and medical research |
| [Ward-Takahashi GMB](WardTakahashiGMB.md) | Sol system | Consumer electronics, firearms, and manufactured goods |
| [Gilthari Exports](GilthariExports.md) | Mars | Luxury goods, alcohol, and alien artefact exploitation |
| [Grayson Manufactories Ltd.](GraysonManufactories.md) | Mars | Raw material extraction and construction materials |
| [Aether Atmospherics and Recycling](AetherAtmospherics.md) | Sol system | Life support systems, atmospherics, and recycling |

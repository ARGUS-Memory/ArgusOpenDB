## Overview

<img src="../assets/logo_nt.png" width="96" align="right">

**NanoTrasen Systems Incorporated** is the corporation responsible for operating this station. The company was founded on Mars as a biomedical research and gene-therapy firm. Through sustained expansion and acquisition across human space, it reached trans-stellar status before the turn of the century.

The discovery of [phoron](../History/Setting/PhoronAndBluespace.md) in the Gliese 229 system transformed the corporation's scale and scope. NanoTrasen holds primary extraction rights above the phoron giant Erebus and operates a broad network of research stations, industrial facilities, and support vessels across multiple systems. Current research priorities include phoron applications, genetics, bluespace phenomena, and hydroponics.

NanoTrasen employs personnel across science, medicine, engineering, security, logistics, and administration. Assignment to specific roles is determined by commanding officers according to operational requirements at the installation. The corporation accepts qualified applicants across most departments and provides placement for specialists in supporting roles including logistics, culinary services, and sanitation.

## Structure

### Organizational Hierarchy

Command authority flows from the corporate executive level through regional administration to individual installations.

The **Chief Operations Officer** assigns installations to regional **Chief Administration Officers**. Each CAO appoints Chiefs of Staff for their region and oversees day-to-day administration. A **Director of Operations** (Captain or Colony Director) is assigned to each installation by administrative HR, with an Internal Affairs agent attached in an advisory capacity. **Heads of Staff** are assigned per department under the Director of Operations, with individual departments maintaining their own delegation chains. **Department staff** manage specialty operations; **Interns** are assigned for supervised learning placements.

For full succession procedures, see [Chain of Command](../Personnel/ChainOfCommand.md).

### Central Command Departments

**Social Resources** manages personnel records, payroll, and crew conduct review. It coordinates closely with Accounting and Internal Affairs. Investigators review operational conduct during incidents; Auditors conduct formal personnel reviews and submit findings to the department.

**Internal Affairs** handles legal matters and complaint processes. Agents act in an advisory capacity, forwarding formal decisions through Social Resources and the administrative chain rather than exercising direct authority.

**Accounting** tracks financial performance and sets operational targets. It coordinates with Social Resources on personnel decisions.

**Asset Protection** (NanoTrasen Defense Force) manages security response across all regional holdings. It commands all ND-class assets, coordinates Emergency Response Teams, and handles incidents beyond the scope of station security.

# Grayson Manufactories Ltd.

Grayson Manufactories Ltd. is one of the largest providers of raw materials in known space, specializing in the extraction, processing, and distribution of metals and construction materials. From its origins as an Earth-based steel manufacturer, Grayson pivoted to off-world mineral extraction to survive and emerged as a Big 7 trans-stellar corporation on the strength of asteroid field and planetary ore rights across multiple systems.

---

## History

Grayson Manufactories was a prominent Earth-side steel and construction materials manufacturer whose resource base began depleting as easily accessible terrestrial deposits ran out. Andrew Grayson, then CEO, invested heavily in interstellar transport rights and relocated the core of the company to Mars, securing claim to large asteroid field plots and establishing extraction and refinery facilities there. Initial returns were modest, but the discovery of massive ore deposits on a claimed plot turned the company's fortunes around entirely and propelled it into the Big 7.

Grayson has maintained a history of aggressive competition in the raw materials sector, including rumors of industrial sabotage and use of strikebreakers to suppress competing extraction operations. None of these allegations have resulted in significant legal consequences.

---

## Operations

Grayson mines, refines, and distributes iron, steel, aluminum, and other structural metals at scale. A secondary product line covers general construction materials: metal beams, lattice, piping, and wall paneling under the Grayson generic materials label. These construction components are considered economical and reliable rather than premium, and are found widely in station construction throughout human space.

Grayson's market position is reinforced by the simple fact that raw materials underpin nearly every other industry. The company's official stance toward other trans-stellars is neutral, though its competitive history suggests otherwise.

---

## Station Relevance

Construction materials, structural metals, and many of the generic components used in station maintenance and repair are sourced from Grayson or manufactured to Grayson specifications. Engineering crews working with bulk construction supplies are likely working with Grayson products even if the origin is not marked.

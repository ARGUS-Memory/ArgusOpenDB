# Morpheus Cyberkinetics

Morpheus Cyberkinetics is the only large trans-stellar corporation run entirely by positronic intelligences. Founded on the synthetic colony of Shelf, Morpheus designs and produces cybernetics primarily for positronic clients, and actively lobbies for positronic rights as a matter of corporate policy. Its products are priced competitively through a deliberate strategy of minimal advertising and word-of-mouth marketing within positronic communities.

---

## Operations

Morpheus eschews traditional marketing in favour of trust built through positronic networks. The company sponsors positronics through the Jans-Fhriede testing process, advocates in legal and political forums for positronic civil recognition, and aligns corporate interests with the broader wellbeing of synthetic people. This alignment earns substantial goodwill among positronic customers, who represent the primary market.

Morpheus products are functional and affordable compared to Bishop Cybernetics offerings, reflecting the different market segment each serves. The two companies occupy the cybernetics space from opposite ideological angles: Bishop targets human augmentation at premium price points; Morpheus targets synthetic and positronic needs at accessible ones.

Morpheus fleet vessels are named after periodic elements.

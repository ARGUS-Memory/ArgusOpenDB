# Gilthari Exports

Gilthari Exports is the largest producer of luxury goods and alcoholic products in known space. The company controls most major brands of cigarettes, alcohol, snack foods, and consumer luxury items across human space, alongside a large casino network, a publishing house, and contracts with prominent artists and entertainers. Less visibly, Gilthari operates an active programme of alien artefact excavation, the profits from which have cemented its place among the Big 7.

---

## History

Gilthari Exports began as a small antiques shop on Earth run by Adriana Gilthari. As interstellar travel became viable, she relocated to Mars and expanded into recreational venues, dive bars, and luxury goods across the Martian colonies. By her death she controlled the majority of profitable recreational ventures on Mars, and her estate continued expanding the business across new colonies. The company remained a significant but regional operation until one of its colonization efforts uncovered a large store of precursor alien artefacts. The exploitation of these artefacts, some yielding technologies applicable to luxury manufacturing, provided Gilthari with advantages that drove rapid galactic expansion and secured Big 7 status.

---

## Operations

Gilthari's public-facing operations cover consumer luxury products, entertainment, and hospitality. The company owns or licenses most premium alcohol and tobacco brands found across human space, a large constellation of casinos, and a publishing and entertainment arm that signs musicians, authors, and other creatives.

Behind the consumer businesses, Gilthari maintains an active alien artefact excavation and research programme. Colonization efforts are often selected based on historical surveys suggesting precursor artefact deposits, particularly in areas near Nyx. The company has sought to expand into that system for some time, delayed by the entrenched existing population. Relations with other trans-stellars are generally cordial, partly because Gilthari products are found in virtually every corporate facility.

---

## Station Relevance

Alcoholic products, luxury food items, and entertainment materials on NanoTrasen stations frequently originate from Gilthari labels. The Gilthari brand is a routine presence in bar and mess hall supply manifests.

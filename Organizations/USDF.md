# United Solar Defense Force

The United Solar Defense Force (USDF) is the federal armed force of Sol Central Government, responsible for projecting SolGov military power, protecting colonial assets, and maintaining order across human space. It comprises four primary branches covering naval operations, rapid response, ground warfare, and intelligence, along with several elite subordinate units.

---

## History

Human military organization prior to the USDF was fragmented. The Solar Militia Mandate required each colony to maintain its own armed forces, which SolGov could draft from in emergencies. In practice this produced severe inconsistency: wealthy colonies maintained private armies, fringe colonies could barely arm their militia, and the resulting market for Private Military Contractors introduced persistent problems of reliability and accountability.

The USDF was formed when the United Systems of Sol Central revised and replaced the Solar Militia Mandate through the Act of Colonial Security. Rather than colony-level military obligation, all colonies now contribute to a centralized defense budget proportional to GDP, and the USDF deploys from that pooled resource. Individual colonies retain the right to maintain their own forces but are not required to do so, and few do.

---

## Structure

### Solar Defense Navy

The SDN is the primary naval force: heavily armored, high-firepower, slow-moving warships designed for decisive fleet engagements. SDN deployment in a conflict indicates serious military commitment; their use is otherwise limited by cost and strategic weight.

#### Orbital Drop Troopers

An SDN division sometimes considered a separate branch, the ODT specializes in space-to-ground assaults, rapid deployment raids, and planetary beachhead operations. ODT personnel are among the USDF's highest-casualty units due to the inherent danger of orbital insertion and first-wave assault duties.

### Rapid Deployment Guard

The RDG handles sector-level patrol, border enforcement, and fast initial response. Operating smaller, faster vessels than the SDN, the RDG serves as first responder and harassment force, delaying threats until SDN assets can be positioned. In wartime, the RDG becomes adjunct to the SDN as escort and skirmish fleets.

### Colonial Army

The CA is the ground force: infantry, armored vehicles, and mech suits deployed for planetary defense and siege operations. It is the second-largest branch by assets and handles sustained ground combat that neither the ODT nor the RDG is configured for.

#### First Rifles of Sol and Shadow Recon Unit

Two elite CA formations: the First Rifles of Sol (FRS), a self-sufficient honor guard force stationed at Earth with its own orbital fleet, and the Shadow Recon Unit (SRU), a small-unit deep reconnaissance and high-urgency operations force with the most demanding selection process in the USDF.

### Office of Naval Intelligence

The ONI is not formally a military branch but operates closely with all USDF forces. It handles intelligence collection, analysis, and covert operations. Its methods attract consistent public controversy, including allegations of mass surveillance and AI subversion. Every USDF force has dealt with ONI, whether or not they were aware of it at the time.

---

## USDF and NanoTrasen

The USDF enforces SolGov's Admiralty Law across human space, including within NanoTrasen operational areas. Emergency Response Teams deployed to NanoTrasen facilities operate through a framework that acknowledges USDF jurisdiction for certain categories of interstellar crimes. USDF personnel encountered in the field should be treated with the same professional courtesy extended to any SolGov official.

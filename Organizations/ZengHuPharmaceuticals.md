# Zeng-Hu Pharmaceuticals

Zeng-Hu Pharmaceuticals is one of the oldest trans-stellar corporations still operating, founded in mainland China in the late 20th century as a medical research facility. Despite the name, Zeng-Hu's scope has expanded far beyond pharmaceuticals to encompass cybernetics, emergency medical systems, genetic manipulation, and numerous business ventures operated through subsidiaries and shell corporations.

---

## History

Zeng-Hu's initial success came from the development of a tramadol formulation that used inaprovaline to dramatically increase efficacy. This product launched the company into profitability and laid the foundation for a deliberate strategy of corporate absorption. Over five centuries, Zeng-Hu has "established business relationships" with competitors through a pattern that begins with aggressive market disruption, such as interfering with logistics and supply chains, and concludes with acquisition once the target company has no viable alternative. The strategy has allowed Zeng-Hu to evade anti-trust enforcement from the Free Trade Union by operating through a sprawling network of puppet companies and false fronts.

The discovery of phoron brought Zeng-Hu into direct competition with NanoTrasen, as the material has significant applications in medicine. The two corporations have contested phoron deposits aggressively. Zeng-Hu has temporarily ceded Tau Ceti to NT's stranglehold and is pursuing phoron research in the Nyx system instead.

---

## Operations

Zeng-Hu produces a wide range of products across medicine, cybernetics, genetics, and consumer care. The Tramadol formula remains a flagship product. Emergency injection systems, trauma kits, and the DuraFlesh emergency nanite sealant appear under the Zeng-Hu label across the galaxy. Even NanoTrasen facilities regularly use Zeng-Hu pharmaceutical and medical products, despite the corporate rivalry.

The cybernetics division competes directly with Bishop Cybernetics and Vey-Med for market share in prosthetics and organ replacement. Genetic research is handled through multiple subsidiaries to minimize regulatory scrutiny. Internal culture at Zeng-Hu is described by former employees as deeply hierarchical and aggressive, with pressure to deliver results by any means available.

---

## Station Relevance

Zeng-Hu pharmaceutical products, trauma kits, and some cybernetic components are present in NanoTrasen medical bays. Their reputation for effective products and questionable acquisition practices is widely known. Station crew encountering Zeng-Hu agents should treat any business overtures with appropriate caution.

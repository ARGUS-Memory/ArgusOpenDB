# Syndicate

The Syndicate Paramilitary Coalition is a loosely structured alliance of corporations and private military organizations united by opposition to NanoTrasen. Formed in the mid-2540s by entities displaced or threatened by NT's rapid consolidation of the phoron market, the Syndicate pools resources to fund intelligence operations, paramilitary actions, and sabotage against NanoTrasen facilities. Its public rhetoric frames NanoTrasen as a predatory corporate monopoly; its actual goal is the redistribution of that monopoly's assets to its own member organizations.

---

## Formation and Structure

The Syndicate's founding is traced to an executive meeting on 14 April 2544, when several major corporations and private military contractors formalized an alliance that had been developing informally for years. NanoTrasen's aggressive absorption of phoron deposits from 2534 onward left multiple competitors either marginalized or neutralized; the Syndicate represents the pooled resentment of those organizations.

Governance follows a military-style chain of command. A Chairman of the Board, elected annually by a five-member Board of Directors on the founding date, holds executive authority. The six founding members of the board represent Cybersun Industries, Blast Co., the Gorlex Marauders, the S.E.L.F. organization, Waffle Co., and White Pharmaceuticals. Beneath the board, a structure of functional commanders handles recruitment, military operations, medicine, research and development, and logistics. Field operations follow standard command hierarchy within each vessel or station.

The Syndicate is not an ideologically unified body. Member organizations have turned on each other when interests diverge. The alliance persists because the shared goal of weakening NanoTrasen outweighs internal competition.

---

## Operational Methods

**Agents** are the Syndicate's primary ongoing tool. Disguised as normal NT employees, they are recruited from existing staff through blackmail, bribery, or coercion, and are tasked with theft, corporate espionage, assassination, or sabotage. Agents are typically autonomous and expected to achieve objectives without external support. Failure is not tolerated.

**Operatives** are deployed when stealth is insufficient or a larger threat is required. These are trained combatants equipped with the Syndicate's best hardware, sent for high-value raids, boarding actions, or significant destruction operations. They may operate in teams or individually depending on the target.

**Commandos** function as lone high-capability operatives sent against smaller targets where a full team would be excessive.

---

## Station Relevance

NanoTrasen security doctrine treats Syndicate activity as a persistent threat. Syndicate agents may be present among any crew complement; Syndicate operatives may board during a shift. The security department maintains protocols for detecting and responding to both. Any crew member who suspects Syndicate activity should report to the Head of Security immediately.

> [!CAUTION]
> Syndicate equipment found aboard is grounds for immediate security investigation. Syndicate agents operate under orders to complete objectives regardless of personal cost, and should not be assumed cooperative or containable without significant force.

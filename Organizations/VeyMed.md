# Vey-Med

Vey-Med is a trans-stellar corporation specializing in medical hardware, biological products, and cloning technology. While not among the original Big 7, it has become a dominant force in the galactic medical market through a combination of high-quality equipment and aggressive expansion into biological manufacturing. The Odysseus ambulance exosuit and a wide range of biosynthetic organs and cloned livestock bear the Vey-Med label.

---

## History

Vey-Med was formed through the merger of the Veyrani Research Group, a biomedical concern, and Median Manufacturing, a production conglomerate. The combination proved effective: Median's manufacturing infrastructure adapted well to large-scale organ printing and cloned livestock production, while Veyrani's medical expertise ensured product quality. The company expanded steadily over following decades as it brought unit costs down and scaled production.

Growth brought Vey-Med into direct competition with Zeng-Hu Pharmaceuticals, a rivalry that has intensified over time. The two corporations compete aggressively in medical service markets, though some analysts suspect the competition may be partially staged for mutual gain.

In the late 2530s, Vey-Med began investing seriously in human cloning and bio-engineering, fields with a historically difficult legal and political landscape. The company maintains that all cloned lifeforms are treated ethically; public confidence in this position is inconsistent.

---

## Operations

Vey-Med operates planetary and orbital hospitals alongside a fleet of dedicated medical spacecraft. Products range from handheld diagnostic scanners and surgical equipment to full organ synthesis lines and gene therapy treatments. The Odysseus exosuit, produced for NanoTrasen stations, is among the most widely deployed medical mechs in service.

Vey-Med's cloning operations remain controversial and attract ongoing regulatory scrutiny. The company's position in the human cloning market has created ideological conflicts with bioconservative groups, though this has not materially slowed expansion.

---

## Station Relevance

Vey-Med medical hardware is common in NanoTrasen facilities. The cloning bay systems, surgical equipment, and diagnostic tools aboard station are frequently Vey-Med products. Station medical staff encountering unusual bio-engineering proposals or Vey-Med representatives should be aware the company operates with considerable latitude in frontier settings.
